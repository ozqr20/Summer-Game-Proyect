Description:

Tetris: Slot the blocks falling into the correct spaces between to create a row and earn points. Stack 4 rows to earn maximum points called a tetris!

#: Decide the position of your battleships and try to guess the position of your oppents ships. Destroying all your opponents ships earns you a victory royale!

Hangman: Try to guess letter by letter what word is shown in front of you with only the amount of letters being revealed to you!

8 Ball pool: Hit the striped or solid balls(decided by whoever hits the first ball in) into the 6 holes available. Knocking all your type of ball in plus the 8 ball earns you a victory royale!
